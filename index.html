About of Art

A doodler is someone who creates spontaneous and often repetitive drawings, typically in the margins of notebooks, on scraps of paper, or even on digital devices. Doodling is often done absentmindedly or during periods of idle time. However, some artists specialize in doodle art, creating intricate and detailed designs that may incorporate patterns, shapes, and characters.

Details

Doodling can serve as a form of creative expression, stress relief, or a way to explore ideas. Colored Pencil Artist: Colored pencil artists use colored pencils as their primary medium to create artwork. Colored pencils offer a wide range of colors and can be blended and layered to achieve various effects. Artists may use colored pencils to create realistic drawings, vibrant illustrations, or abstract compositions.

Artist

Techniques such as shading, blending, and burnishing are commonly employed to achieve depth and texture in colored pencil artwork. Colored pencil artists may work on paper or other surfaces suitable for colored pencil application. Both doodlers and colored pencil artists explore their creativity through different means, and each brings their unique style and approach to their artwork.
